Our Story

Born from a Love for Naples

✦

Napoletana Nostra was born from a deep love for Neapolitan tradition — the kind that starts before dawn when the dough is made, and ends late at night when the last pizza leaves the oven.

We are nestled on Rruga Marin Barleti in the heart of Prizren, and every detail of our space — from the herb pots on the terrace windowsills to the glow of our wood-fired oven — is designed to transport you to Naples.

Our dough ferments for 72 hours, our tomatoes come from San Marzano, and our oven burns olive wood at 480°C. That is not a recipe — it is a ritual.
